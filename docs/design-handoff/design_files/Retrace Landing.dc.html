why this is different

No app sees the full picture. Retrace connects the dots.

Your life doesn't happen on one platform. A single decision might span a ChatGPT brainstorm, a WhatsApp conversation with a friend, and an Obsidian note where you planned next steps. Retrace is the only place where all three connect.

rediscovery

It remembers what you forgot.

An idea you mentioned once in January, forgot, then brought up again in April. Retrace sees the thread even when you don't. Quiet cards surface what was happening this time last year. Nothing nudges you, the insights simply wait until you come back.

privacy by architecture

Intimate by default, not by promise.

Your data is stored locally

Your conversations are structured and indexed on your own machine. No telemetry, no tracking.

You choose what leaves

Deep reflection uses Claude, and it sees only what you allow. Labels decide exactly what's shared.

No engagement bait

No badges, no red dots, no notifications. Open source, MIT licensed. Insights wait quietly until you return.

The patterns are already there. Retrace just helps you see them.

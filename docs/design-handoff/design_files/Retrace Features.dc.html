Connect · MCP

Bring your favorite AI to your own data

Retrace exposes your context layer over MCP, so any AI app can read it: Claude, Codex, a local CLI, or a model you run yourself. Ask about patterns, draft a reflection, generate an artifact. You choose the tool, and you choose what it sees.
